Get involved!

TampereJS is a community of web developers and a regular meetup revolving around JavaScript and web development in general. As the name implies, it takes place in Tampere, Finland. It is organized on first Thursday of every month, with different talks, sponsor and location every time. So, if you are a web developer (or just web-curious) TampereJS is the place to be!

We are already over 400 strong and growing. Come join us for pizza and a drink or two!

TampereJS sponsor guidelines

We thank you for your contribution to the community Here are few tips and guidelines to help you, as a sponsor, to get most out of TampereJS.

Before the event
  • Send organizers the address to the location (preferrably in city centrum) and number of attendees your space can handle (minimum of 20 + organizers)
  • Preferrably have a JavaScript related talk ready
  • Tweet, Facebook, Snapchat like a MF (remember to mention TampereJS so people know it's a community event)
Just before/during the event
  • Have TampereJS signs leading from the street address all the way to the actual location where talks are held
  • Have someones phone number on the first sign in case someone doesn't find their way or doors are locked
  • Finnish are pretty quiet so have some music in the background while waiting for the talks to begin and after the talks
  • People start appearing 30 minutes before the event so be ready at that point (signs, snacks, drinks)
  • Have drinks and snacks available at the beginning so people can eat & drink while they wait
  • Have some vegetarian and non-alcoholic options
  • First talks usually start 15-25 minutes past the starting time, once everyone has arrived
  • You can have short company presentation (max. 5 minutes) at the beginning and you get the first talk
  • Remember to tweet @TampereJS #TampereJS so we can retweet you
  • When the talks are over, there will be networking/mingling for hour/few hours so prepare for that
  • If you have a favorite bar nearby, you can recommend that as a "continuation place" after leaving the premises (usually bunch of people will have few drinks after the meetup)
After the event
  • Post the presentation decks or link to the community in Facebook & Twitter
